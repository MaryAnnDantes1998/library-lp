LIBRARIAN'S MESSAGE

"Welcome to the Information Resource Center and Library of the Saint Francis of Assisi College(SFAC), Bayanan Campus. The Center/Library is committed to uphold its Vision/Mission of providing relevant, up-to-date and Valuable information materials in support to our school's objectives of providing quality education to its students. Books and other information materials are well-organized and processed to make them searchable and retrievable. In view of the modern trend, we maintained a Library and Information Management System where in bibliographic details of these materials can be accessed via our campus Online Public Access Catalogue(OPAC) The Library still maintained the card catalogues as back-up-file, in casethe system will have a trouble in the operation.


To substantiate with our collected information resources, the Department of Science and Technology(DOST) through its Science and Technology Information Institute(STII) has provided our school with a kiosk for their Starbook. The Starbook serves as portal to the digitized information on science, technology, mathematics, and related fields. It consist of bibliographic data and texts from varios sources. We, in the Information Resource Center encourage all the students,teachers, and staffof the Saint Francis of Assisi College to visit out Library so that you, too, can benefit from our available wealth of resources."

Jovencia Garcia,

Saint Francis of Assisi College, Head Librarian
